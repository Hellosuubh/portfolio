Hello, I'm Shubham Raj, a dynamic and versatile professional with expertise in both full-stack web development and graphic design, specializing in UI/UX. With a passion for creating seamless and visually appealing digital experiences, I bring a unique blend of technical proficiency and creative flair to every project. As a full-stack web developer, I am adept at crafting robust and user-friendly applications, employing a wide range of programming languages and frameworks. On the design front, my specialty lies in UI/UX, where I leverage my graphic design skills to create aesthetically pleasing and intuitive interfaces. My portfolio showcases a diverse array of projects that reflect my commitment to innovation, functionality, and user satisfaction. Whether it's coding the backend of a powerful web application or designing an engaging user interface, I approach each task with enthusiasm and a keen eye for detail. Explore my portfolio to witness the seamless synergy of technology and design in action.

I am a versatile individual with a diverse range of hobbies that reflect my passion for both the analytical and creative aspects of life.
